Bakers Bench strives to make the best pastries, vegan or otherwise. Our pastries are baked fresh every morning in a kitchen shared with dairy, nuts, soy and eggs.

Please follow us on Instagram for more updates. We can't wait to see you in our new home.

Owner and baker Jennifer Yee was born and raised in the San Gabriel Valley. After working at multiple Michelin-starred kitchens across the country (The French Laundry, Jean-Georges, Gilt and more), she returned to LA in 2018 and became the pastry chef of Konbi (2019 Bon Appétit’s Best New Restaurant). After making many of Konbi’s famed croissants, she turned her attention to another challenge: making the best vegan croissant.
